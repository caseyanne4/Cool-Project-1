What is The Office?

A hilarious show about a paper company in Scranton, Pennsylvania. The show is formatted to be like a documentary, so the characters can often address the camera directly. The camera crew captures some amazing moments on screen. With characters such as the clueless boss, Michael Scott; the assistant to the regional manager, Dwight Schrute; and so many other realistic and interesting characters, the show is always fun to watch.

Spoiler Alert!

Some of the characters come and go. Even the position of regional manager is filled by many after Michael. But this show is unlike many comedies: Season 1 is actually worse than Season 9, the last season. The writers manage to maintain the same or better levels of quality and hilarity throughout the show.
